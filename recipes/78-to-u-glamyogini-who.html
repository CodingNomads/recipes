I doubled the recipe and used two cans of corn, instead of grilled corn.

2 cans Trader Joe’s corn

1 tablespoon butter

2 tablespoons vegan mayo

Salt and pepper to taste

Chili powder to taste

2 tablespoons sriracha

1 jalapeño chopped fine with seeds

1/2 red onion chopped fine

1 huge bunch cilantro (I love cilantro)

1/2 lemon juice

1 whole lime

1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Put it in the refrigerator so all the flavors can meld together, and will eat it in a few hours!
