Homemade gnocchi for less than $.50 per person

by Doesntpayfullprice

You can also watch the recipe video here if you prefer: https://youtu.be/4vkjlM7p2bo

2lbs russet potatoes

1 egg

1 1/2 Cups all purpose flour

2t salt.

Peel and cube the potatoes to an even size and boil for 15-20 minutes until fork tender

strain potatoes and pass through a potato ricer, or mash until no lumps remain.

let potatoes cool for 15 minutes, and then add egg, salt and flour, mix with your hands to combine until a it all comes together to form a dough.

Separate dough into two and work with one half at a time rolling into even cylinders about 1/2 inch in diameter.

Cut into 1 inch long pieces.

Either poke a divot in the center with your index finger, or roll against a gnocchi board or fork to shape.

Cook immediately or freeze on a sheet pan and then move into a zip top bag for storage.
