GARLIC CHEESE BOMBS

. .

INGREDIENTS:

1 can pizza dough

6 mozzarella string cheese sticks, cut into 24 pieces (I used a light version)

4 tablespoons butter

1 1/2 tablespoon garlic bread sprinkle (or garlic powder)

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

.

￼

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Spray a 10" cast iron pan with cooking spray and set aside

  2. Roll out pizza dough onto a lightly floured surface and form into a 12" x 8" rectangle

  3. Cut into 24 even squares and place 1 piece of cheese into the center.

  4. Roll up cheese into dough ball and pinch sides to seal.  Set into prepared pan.

  5. Melt butter and stir in seasonings.  Spread onto the tops of the dough.

  6. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.

.

. NOTES:

This may be obvious but I forgot to completely defrost the dough making it very difficult to roll flat with a rolling pin. So yea... defrost the dough!!

Serve with marinara sauce!!! Yay.
