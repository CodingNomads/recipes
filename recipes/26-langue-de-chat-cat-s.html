Langue de Chat ("cat's tongue") Cookies!

by green_amethyst

Just 1 egg white, and equal weight sugar, flour, & butter makes a whole sheet pan of these. They're great solutions for dealing with extra egg whites. Sandwich them with some melted chocolate, and you got copycat Milano cookies!

Step-by-Step Video for following along

Ingredients (makes about 16-20 pieces):  

  • 1 egg white (~30g)   
  • 30g sugar (2 tbsp)
  • 30g butter, melted (2tbsp)
  • 15g flour  (2 tbsp)
  • 15g ground almond (2tbsp)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla paste or extract 

Instruction: 

  1. Mix together all ingredients until thoroughly combined. 
  2. Pipe the batter out to any shape you like.
  3. Bake at 375F for 9~10 minutes, until edges are browned. 
  4. Optional shaping: The cookies can be bend while hot. You can press them on a rolling pin and let them cool; they will harden as they cool.
