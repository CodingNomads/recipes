Strawberry Souffle

by 20secondsvid

Strawberry Souffle Recipe:

video instruction: https://youtu.be/jCxuDpUSpmc

Ingredients:

120g strawberries 35g sugar 1tsp lemon juice 1 tbsp corn starch 2 egg white (medium size) a pinch of salt 30g sugar powdered sugar for decoration

Instruction:

  1. Strawberry base

Blend chopped strawberries with sugar and lemon juice

Strain the puree using a sieve

Put it on medium heat and stir in corn starch

Remove from heat when it becomes thicken and let it cool down

  1. The meringue

Whisk 2 room temperature egg white with a pinch of salt

When it's foamy, start to add total 30g sugar in several times

Whisk to soft peaks then fold to combine with the strawberry base

Bake at 180C for 12 minutes

Serve right away
