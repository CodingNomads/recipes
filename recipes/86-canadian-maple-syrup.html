Canadian Maple Syrup Cookies (Recipe)

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (305g) flour
    • If using Gluten Free flour, add 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1/2 cup (48g) almond flour
  • 1/2 cups (100g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup (113g) salted butter
  • 3/4 cup (255g) maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. In one bowl, add 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar, 1/2 cup (115g) granulated sugar, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 1/2 cup (113g) butter, 3/4 cup (255g) maple syrup (these are the wet ingredients)
  2. With a whisk or a handheld mixer, mix all of the ingredients together until well blended
  3. Add 1 egg, and mix again
  4. In a separate bowl, add 2 1/4 cups (305g) flour, 1/2 (48g) almond flour, 1 tsp baking soda, and 1/2 tsp salt (these are the dry ingredients)
  5. Mix all of the dry ingredients together
  6. Slowly pour 1/4 portions of the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, until fully combined
  7. Gently stir all of the ingredients together until just blended
  8. Take out golf ball sized portions of dough, and roll them together between your palms
  9. Line a baking pan with either parchment paper - OR - cooking spray
  10. Place dollar-sized balls of dough on the pan
  11. Bake the biscuits in the oven at 350Â°F (180Â°C) for 9-10 minutes
  12. Allow the biscuits to cool for a minimum of 15 minutes
  13. If desired, top the cookies with an Maple Syrup Glaze for that extra oomph
  14. Enjoy!
