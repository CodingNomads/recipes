[Homemade] Spicy Chicken Sandwich with Poutine

by hayjac23

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Brioche Bun
  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 1/2 Cup of corn starch
  • 1 Cup of buttermilk
  • 1/2 Cup of mayo
  • 3 Teaspoons garlic powder
  • 3 Teaspoons onion powder
  • 3 Teaspoons paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon hot sauce (i used franks)
  • Leaf Lettuce
  • Pickle
  • French Fries
  • Brown Gravy
  • Mozzarella Curds/Cheese Curds

Directions for Chicken Sandwich

  1. Chicken breast sliced in half to have a thinner piece and then pound with either your hand or a mallet
  2. In a large bowl mix buttermilk, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of onion powder, 1 teaspoon of paprika, add kosher salt, and pepper
  3. Marinate for as long as desired (the longer the better)
  4. In another large bowl put a cup of flour, 1/2 cup of corn starch, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of onion powder, 1 teaspoon of paprika, and 1 teaspoon of cayenne (or more if you desire more heat it doesn’t really change any flavor)
  5. Take chicken out of buttermilk mix and coat in the dry mix and push the mix into the chicken to make sure it’s coated well
  6. Deep fry on 350 for 5 ish minutes on each side or until it is golden brown (I usually take it out just before and it’ll continue to brown on the paper towel)
  7. Toast your bun on stovetop with butter
  8. Add your chicken, lettuce, spicy mayo, and pickles and youre done

Directions for Poutine

  1. Fry your french fries
  2. Top with Cheese Curds
  3. Dump brown gravy all over and enjoy
